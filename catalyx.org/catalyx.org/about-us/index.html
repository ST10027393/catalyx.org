Our Company History

1996
Joe White comes to South Africa
Joe White comes to South Africa

Joe White, the visionary behind Catalyx came to South Africa in 1996 from California, USA. Through the youth initiative Youth With a Mission (YWAM), he joined a ship called the Mercy Ship from Madagascar.

At the age of 18, he originally planned to fill a gap year, but instead his philanthropic work led him to fall in love with KZN and its people.

Joe lived in a township within the Valley of a 1 000 Hills where he started numerous community social initiatives, and it was where his non-profit work was birthed.

2002
Start of World Changers Academy
Start of World Changers Academy

Start of Catalyx

Joe recognized the need to support corporates in understanding and fulfilling their social economic responsibilities and together with his best friend, Karabo Che Mokoape. Catalyx Consulting started in 2011.

Dec 2020
Acquisition of Mindgame Escape
Acquisition of Mindgame Escape

In a significant move in December 2020, Catalyx acquired Mindgame Escape, a well-established provider of immersive escape room experiences. By integrating Mindgame Escape's expertise and innovative approach to escape room adventures, Catalyx aimed to enrich its offerings, offering customers a broader array of immersive and thrilling experiences to enjoy.

Oct 2022
Acquisition of Bluff Personnel
Acquisition of Bluff Personnel

In October 2022, Catalyx acquired Bluff Personnel, a reputable staffing and recruitment agency. This strategic acquisition aimed to bolster Catalyx's position in the market and expand its range of services. By integrating Bluff Personnel's expertise and extensive network, Catalyx sought to enhance its recruitment capabilities and provide comprehensive HR solutions to clients, offering them tailored staffing solutions and facilitating the formation of high-performing teams.

Jun 2023
Start of Afro's Chicken Shop Pavilion
Start of Afro's Chicken Shop Pavilion

In an exciting development in June 2023, Catalyx launched Afro's Chicken Shop Pavilion. This new venture showcased Catalyx's commitment to culinary diversity and innovation. Afro's Chicken Shop Pavilion is a go-to destination for food enthusiasts seeking a vibrant and culturally rich dining experience.

About Catalyx

Who we are

Catalyx is a development consulting firm committed to developing unique solutions to combat social challenges in South Africa. Catalyx offers a variety of complimentary services, both for the public and private sectors. The Catalyx approach to all our projects is rooted in a profound desire to play a meaningful part in meeting the challenges that plague our communities. Therefore, we endeavor at all times for our work to be needs-focused, consensual and sustainable. Our work is guided by a desire for social participation and democratic leadership in the design, implementation, and evaluation of development projects. These can only be achieved through commitment to a high degree of engagement within affected communities, with all relevant stakeholders, in a collaborative and empowering process.
